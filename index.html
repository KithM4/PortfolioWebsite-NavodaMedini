About Me

Pattern Maker & Fashion Designer

Hi, I'm Navoda Medini, a dedicated Pattern Maker and Fashion Designer. With a passion for innovative design and a meticulous approach to pattern making, I create unique, stylish garments that stand out. My expertise lies in blending creativity with precision, ensuring every piece is both beautiful and functional.

Certificate in Computer Science
